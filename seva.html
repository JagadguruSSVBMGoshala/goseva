Go Seva & Donations

Go-seva is at the heart of this Goshala. It is expressed not merely through financial support, but through conscious participation in the daily care, nourishment, and well-being of the cows.

Every contribution made here is directed towards sustaining essential activities that ensure dignity, health, and protection for the cows entrusted to this Goshala.

Forms of Go Seva

Seva at the Goshala takes many forms, each addressing a fundamental aspect of care. These include daily feeding, medical assistance, shelter maintenance, and special care for aged or injured cows.

Devotees may choose to support a specific form of seva or contribute generally towards the ongoing needs of the Goshala.

Daily Annadānam

Providing nutritious food is a daily and essential responsibility. Contributions towards Annadānam support fodder procurement and ensure that cows receive timely and adequate nourishment.

Medical & Protective Care

Medical seva includes regular health check-ups, treatment of injuries, and preventive care. Special attention is given to elderly and rescued cows requiring long-term support.

Offering Seva

Donations may be offered as an expression of gratitude, prayer, or commitment to Go-rakshana. All contributions are utilized solely for the maintenance and welfare of the cows.

Details regarding donation methods are provided below. Devotees are encouraged to contribute according to their capacity, with sincerity rather than obligation.

Seva acknowledgements may be shared upon request. The Goshala remains committed to transparency and responsible stewardship of all offerings.
